Sign Board

Acrylic is a suitable material for indoor and outdoor use. Most often they used as reception signs or signage in an office. Due to its transparency, acrylic is a modern alternative to rigid signs.

The primary purpose of signs is to communicate, to convey information to help the recipient make a decision based on the information provided. Alternatively, advertising signs can used to convince the recipient of the benefits of a particular good or service.

LED signs are extremely reliable and last longer than conventional signs, 3 to 6 years longer than neon and fluorescent signs. LED Signs do not contain gasses that can escape and cause obscuration. They also achieve a better brightness level during their lifetime.

3D signboard - Also known as dimensional signage, 3D signage adds dimensional elements such as raised graphics, logos and letters. With this type of signage, the graphics protrude from the base and have more depth than the 2D alternative with flat graphics.

Road signs divided into three basic categories: Regulatory, Warning, and Guide signs. Regulatory signs tell you what you may or may not do according to the law. The regulations on these signs tell you about speed limits, the direction of traffic, no turning, no parking, etc.

Warning signs inform you of conditions on or off the road. They warn you of potential hazards or changes in roadway conditions. Warning signs are usually diamond-shaped with black symbols or words on a yellow background.

Guide signs provide information about intersections, help you find your way around cities and towns, and point out points of interest along the highway. Guide signs also help you find hospitals, service and gas stations, restaurants, and motels. As a rule, these signs are horizontal rectangles.

There are four types of wayfinding signs: Identification, directional, informational, and regulatory. ANSI Z535 recognizes five different types of signs: Danger signs, Warning signs, Caution signs, notice signs, and Safety instruction signs.

PVC signs are usually known as plastic signs designed for short-term outdoor signage or long-term indoor signage.
