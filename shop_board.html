Create a successful business sign

Successful business sign is a key to attracting as much attention as possible. A graphic designer who specializes in printed materials has compiled some tips for creating custom signs that attract customers;

  • Distinctive Colors - Brands are so distinctive that I have to show you the logos so you can visualize the colors. If you are creating a sign for a sale, red color is the best.
  • The bigger the better - when making your own store sign, the size of the signage and text matters.
    • For your message, it’s best to keep it short and sweet.
    • For the sign above the door, the name of your business often enough, but a short slogan or headline can also suffice.
    • For advertising, keep the message as short as possible - “Now on Sale”, “Open for Business!”. The smaller your message, the larger you can make the font.
  • Choose the right type of signs - cardboard signs, foam signs, Correx signs (corrugated plastic material), aluminum signs, and magnetic signs.
  • Include important information - include your business name, logo, address and phone number, directions to your business, website (online business)
  • Effective Contrasts - When using colors in images, make sure that the colors have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1.
