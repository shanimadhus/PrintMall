Flex Printing

Flex printing is a method of capturing and converting original images into different colors and is used in various industries. In addition, the low cost of use along with longer life is driving the flex market and consequently the flex press market.

Flex material is softer and more flexible than vinyl. Flex material can print on and recolored using special industrial printers. These printers basically work like any other inkjet printer, but are specifically designed to use flex material. It is widely use in various forms, such as hoardings, boards, Lit boards, etc. Its weather resistant properties, print quality and durability make it so popular compared to all other available options.

Flex Applications

Custom Shop signs, custom traffic signs, road show displays, real estate signs, retail store signs, business flag banners, exhibition graphics, hoarding, one-way vision print, hoardings, billboards, drop-down banners, floor standees, backlit hoardings and boards, vehicle wraps, bus panel advertising, bus shelter flex, outdoor and indoor banners, and many more.
