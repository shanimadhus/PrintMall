Design

Graphic design is a craft in which professionals create visual content to convey messages. Using visual hierarchy and page layout techniques, designers use typography and imagery to meet specific user needs and focus on the logic of displaying elements in interactive designs to optimize the user experience.

Graphic design is an ancient craft that dates back from Egyptian hieroglyphics to cave paintings at least 17,000 years old. The term originated in the printing industry in 1920, and now encompasses a range of activities, including logo design. Graphic design in this sense is about esthetics and marketing. Graphic designers attract the viewer with images, colors, and typography.

Graphic designers create visual concepts using computer software or by hand to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and engage consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for applications such as advertisements, brochures, magazines and reports.
