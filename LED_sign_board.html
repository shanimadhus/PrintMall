LED Sign Board

LED signs are small digital screens attached to store fronts to announce your business. They stand out from the wall and help passersby easily find the store when they look up. They are similar to traditional signs, but they display much more than just a logo or brand: LED signs also show animated video content.

Advantages of LED signs for Business;

  • Attention grabbing.
  • An advantage over your competition.
  • Variety of choice.
  • Low maintenance costs.
  • Interactive.
  • Ecologically sound.
  • Animation, multi-colour graphics and text.
  • Automatic brightness control.

It’s important to understand these distinctions so that you don’t assume that all LED signs are expensive. In any case, LED digital signs considered expensive because they require special components to make them work. A LED display is a flat panel display that uses a series of light emitting diodes as pixels for a video display. Because of their brightness, they can used outdoors where they’re visible in the sun for shop signs and billboards.

LED panels composed of individual panels, or modules, which make it possible to build the panels to almost any size. Each module contains LEDs arranged in clusters, known as pixels. Pixels are the basic building block of any content. A high-quality color sign will have three diodes per pixel: one red, one green and one blue.

On the other hand, LEDs are shock resistant and generate very little heat, making them completely safe for use. LED signage is also free of harmful components like mercury and phosphor. Most outdoor LED digital signage lasts on average 100,000 hours.

LED neon (faux-neon) signs such as NeonPlus® use neither glass nor neon gas. Instead, they made from acrylic a thermoplastic better known as Altuglas or Perspex which is lightweight, shatter-resistant and effective at transmitting light. Acrylic has a tendency to have the best optical properties for most applications. It provides excellent light transmission, scatters LED hotspots, and is easy and inexpensive to manufacture. Basically, laser engraved signs are engraving designs of your brand logo, name, etc. on a flat surface. The surface can made of any material (Acrylic, Correx, PVC, Aluminum, Glass, wooden, etc.), depending on your choice. The whole engraving process done by laser machine.

LEDs consume much less power than incandescent bulbs, and this also applies to decorative LED lights such as Christmas tree lights.

LED lights emit optical radiation that can, only in certain circumstances potentially damages the eyes and skin depending on several variables that must be considered. These variables include the spectrum (or wavelength distribution) of the LED light source, the intensity of the lighting (particularly in the blue strip). Maybe the greatest disadvantage of LED bulbs is that they emit more blue light than incandescent bulbs, which are more on the red end of the spectrum.
