Hoarding

A Hoarding or Billboard is a large outdoor advertisement typically placed in high-traffic areas, such as along busy roads. Hoarding or Billboards present large advertisements to passing pedestrians and drive by passengers. Brands typically use billboards to build their brands or promote their new products.

Posters are the other common form of billboard advertising, found primarily on major and secondary roads means a public street connecting two or more roads of equal or greater classification. Billboards have a smaller format and seen primarily by residents and passengers drives by, with some pedestrian exposure.
